# 01 · Product Mission

Worth It helps shoppers understand **how a clothing item is likely to wear, what to watch for,
and whether the price makes sense.**

It is a **decision tool, not a fashion taste tool.**

## Out of scope for v1
Worth It should **not** measure:
- Style
- Desirability
- Ethics
- Sustainability
- PFAS
- Total emotional value

These may be considered in future versions, but v1 stays focused on wear, tradeoffs, and price fit.
